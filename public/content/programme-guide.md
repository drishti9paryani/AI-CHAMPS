# AI Champs — Full Programme Guide

Everything you need to know about the 4-week programme. Read through, then pick your path.

---

## Week 1 — Anything You'd Like 🌱

Explore ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini and discover how each one can change the way you work. No deliverables — just open a tool and have a real conversation about something on your plate. Your goal: leave with at least one concrete idea or workflow you couldn't have reached without AI.

**Suggested tools:** ChatGPT (voice mode), Claude.ai, Gemini

---

## Week 2 — Script and Image Generation 🎨

Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT. Refine your ideas into polished scripts and stunning visuals. Learn to write prompts that get you first-draft-ready output on the first try.

**Suggested tools:** Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, DALL·E, Ideogram

---

## Week 3 — AI Influencer / Avatar and Music 🤖

Build a digital persona. HeyGen, Synthesia, ElevenLabs, Suno. Your AI twin is clocking in. This week is about understanding what's possible when you combine voice, face, and motion AI.

**Suggested tools:** HeyGen, Synthesia, ElevenLabs, Suno

---

## Week 4 — Direct a 2-Minute Movie 🎬

Script it, generate it, cut it. Ship a 2-minute AI film by end of week. You have all the tools — now use them together.

**Suggested tools:** Runway, Kling AI, Descript, Higgsfield
