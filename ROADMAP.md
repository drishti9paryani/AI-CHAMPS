# AI Champs V2 — Product Roadmap
> White Rivers Media · whiteriversmedia.tech

---

## Vision
Transform AI Champs from a training portal into a platform people **want** to visit.
Goal: Duolingo + Notion + gaming platform + internal agency innovation hub.

---

## V2 Features (Phase 2 — Post V1 Launch)

### Dashboard Ecosystem (Multi-page)
- **Home** — personalized welcome, rank, recent achievements, team leaderboard snapshot
- **Learning Hub** — AI lessons, prompt library, agency-specific use cases, saved resources
- **Challenges** — weekly/team/cross-team challenges, bonus missions
- **Leaderboards** — agency-wide, team-wise, monthly, quarterly
- **Skill Tree** — visual progression: Prompting → Automation → AI Research → Agent Building → Client Apps → Productivity
- **Rewards Center** — badges, unlockables, milestones, recognition wall
- **Innovation Lab** — submit AI workflows, prompt ideas, automation ideas, case studies
- **Team Zone** — personalized content per department

### Engagement & Gamification
- Streaks & XP system
- Levels + unlockable AI Champion titles
- Hidden achievements & Easter eggs
- Team battles & department rivalries
- AI tournaments & monthly championships
- Seasonal events
- Hall of Fame
- Unlockable avatars
- Agency quests

### Admin Super Access
- Full user override: edit progress, award XP, assign challenges, add achievements, reset modules, recommend content
- Bulk actions on users
- Content management: add lessons, create challenges, assign resources
- Engagement analytics per team/person

### Progress Views (Replace Day View)
- Weekly View
- Monthly Progress View
- Learning Journey View
- Achievement Timeline
- Skill Growth Tracker
